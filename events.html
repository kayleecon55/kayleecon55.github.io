Loyola's Baking Club hosts monthly meetings where group members can gather to bake and decorate baked goods. Meetings are typically hosted in the Fernandez Center on Sundays from 4-6PM. Previous meetings have included cookie decorating, halloween cupcake decorating, and gingerbread house building. Students can register for events through The Bridge.

The Baking Club also hosts bake sales on an as-needed basis. Bake sales are often used as fundraising tools to create new, unique experiences for the club that would otherwise be out of budget. Bake sales are typically conducted in the Student Center, though can take place anywhere based on need.

The Loyola Baking Club is excited to announce that, starting Spring of 2023, members will be offered the opportunity to take baking classes through some of Baltimore's most distinguished bakeries. Here, students can gather and learn tips and techniques from a trained professional.
